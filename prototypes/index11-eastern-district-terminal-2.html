Eastern District Terminal - 2

The south portion of Brooklyn Eastern District Terminal is now Martha P. Johnson East River State Park. To the north, between North 8th and North 9th Street, lies the first part of Bushwick Inlet Park to open to the public â€“ a soccer field constructed in 2010 and community house built in 2013. The community house - located at 86 Kent Avenue - occupies the former site of the headquarters building of BEDT.

North of that and also once part of the rail terminal is the massive blue and white CitiStorage building. It was the last piece of Bushwick Inlet Park to be acquired, following a long battle by community members to push the City to live up to its promise to Greenpoint and Williamsburg. After almost 10 years of holdouts by the owner, inaction by the city and in the face of relentless advocacy by the community, the City finally acquired the property in late 2016. Today, the building is vacant, awaiting funds for demolition, remediation, design and construction.
